DefyPanda

DefyPanda is a panda-club active in the metaverse. All the members are individuals come from different dimensions or universes. Although they have different cultures, backgrounds, languages and even species, they share one thing in common: they defy all the things that try to define them, and they don't give a fuck about what they don't give a fuck about. Have your own DefyPanda to join the party. DefyPanda members have the right to participate in exclusive future activities and games.

We defy anything that tries to define us

DefyPanda is a collection of 9999 NFTs generated by algorithm and designed with attitudes. Each DefyPanda has different traits such as panda-type, bodygears, and headgears. These traits are inspired by animes, movies, and pop culture. Every panda is unique in its own way while some of the traits are a bit rarer.

Your DefyPanda is not only an NFT, it's also your character in the DefyPanda game. The traits of your panda will bring different ingame attributes in the upcoming game.
